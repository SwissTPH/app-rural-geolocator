Rural Geolocator tutorial

This is an application to support geo-referencing of houses using the PyBossa framework on Crowdcrafting.org.

The application loads a satellite image into the web map. You can use the zoom button to have a closer look, and can click and hold the map to pan to different parts of the map segment. However, you will not be able to scroll beyond the segment of the map you are presented with.

When you see a structure that looks like a house inside the orange box, click the black Mark button to enter the marking mode. Click the middle of the structure to mark it. You can move points you placed by entering the edit mode by clicking the Edit button, or delete a mark after clicking Delete. You can only mark houses inside the orange bounding box. If the bounding box runs through a house, mark it only if more than half the area is inside the box. Keep clicking on the map until you have marked all houses, and then save the markers by clicking on the Submit task button.

If there are no houses in the segment you're looking at, simply click the Submit task button.
